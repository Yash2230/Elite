7v7 Match Engine How the dice decide your matches

Every duel on the pitch comes down to two numbers. Same number, defense wins. Different numbers, the gap tells you what happens. This page walks through the full sequence: attack, pass, flank, shot, and set pieces.

Same Number

Both sides pick the identical number. The duel goes to whoever is defending: tackle made, pass intercepted, or shot saved, depending on the phase.

Different Numbers

Subtract the smaller pick from the larger one. That gap is the difference, and the difference is what actually happens next. Read each phase below for what each gap means.
